    Operating Authority & USDOT Number

    Every company that operates commercial vehicles for passenger transport or interstate cargo hauling must register with the FMCSA and obtain a unique USDOT Number. This also applies to commercial intrastate carriers transporting hazardous materials in amounts that require a safety permit. The USDOT Number serves as a unique identifier, used in audits, compliance reviews, crash investigations, and inspections.

    • Applying for a new USDOT Number
    • Updating USDOT registration information
    • Reactivating USDOT after revocation

    UCR Services

    The Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) system is the annual registration mechanism for interstate motor carriers. If you fall into one of the following categories, you'll need UCR:

    • Operations requiring a USDOT number for interstate transportation
    • Operations requiring a MC or MX number from the FMCSA
    • Interstate brokers
    • Interstate leasing companies
    • Interstate freight forwarders
